About M4H

The Affordable Care Act (ACA) continues to serve Montanans and Americans as legislation promoting equity and efficiency in US healthcare. Yet, Senator Steve Daines has attempted numerous times to repeal the ACA, even without a backup healthcare plan. Affordable healthcare is no longer a red-blue issue, rather, it is a humanity issue. The ACA has enabled rural hospitals to continue operating and provided everyday Montanans with access to quality healthcare. We must band together to express that Montanans are in favor of affordable healthcare.

The Health Care Affordability Act of 2021 (House Bill H.R.369) seeks to strengthen the Affordable Care Act by reducing premium costs of health insurances across all income levels. Its implementation would improve healthcare equity through appealing to a more applicable dimension of equality, while also strengthening an already efficient solution to healthcare.
